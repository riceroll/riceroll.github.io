Jianzhe   Gu

Ph.D. Researcher, Engineer, and Designer
jianzhe.gu@gmail.com • riceroll.github.io • Google Scholar
Human-Computer Interaction Institute, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University

Jianzhe obtained his Ph.D. from CMU HCII & Robotics Institute, advised by Lining Yao, and Ding Zhao.

Jianzhe’s research is at the intersection of human-computer interaction, robotics, and machine learning. He develops computational tools and interfaces for robotic design optimization. He has extensive experience with control & design optimization, robotic manipulation & planning, physical simulation. He is highly proficient with programming languages including C/C++, python, javascript and Taichi, and tools including Mujoco, pyTorch and cloud computing.
